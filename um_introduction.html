Introduction

You have chosen or been chosen to work with skarphed as your personal assistant to fight the everyday problems of webmanagement. We are very happy that you want to learn it and hope, this usermanual will teach you quickly how to handle your new tool.

About this manual

This manual consists of sections. In the first section Hello skarphed that we are currently on, we will show you how profiles work and how the skarphed-admin, skarphed's administration GUI is used. The second section Getting started will teach you how to handle your servers and skarphed-core-installations. The third section Inside skarphed will teach you how skarphed-core-installations are being managed and how to build your websites.
